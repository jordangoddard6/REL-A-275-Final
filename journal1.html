Journal #1

I'm very happy to be taking this class right now because I feel like I need it. I have been really apathetic towards the gospel and towards God lately, and I really feel like I'm missing something in my life. It's been hard to find motivation to live the gospel and come to Christ. Satan has done a good job of distracting me from what's important, and I feel like this class is exactly what I need to overcome that.

I'm super grateful for everything that we've been able to learn thus far. I enjoy learning from all sorts of professors and hearing from every one about what they can teach about their area of expertise. I know that there's much more to come and I'm excited for it.

I think I'm most grateful though that this course starting off with some learning about precepts and application. I really need some of that in my life, and I think that was a great first step. I want more than anything to take these precepts and applications seriously so that I can become again the person that I want to be.

All information is Jordan's opinion and is not representative of the Church or of BYU
